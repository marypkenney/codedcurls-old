A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Toronto, Ontario, with my small growing family. My journey into tech started with exposure to a full website build at the company I was working at and I became fascinated with the work. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific needs. I became invested in finding a way to learn the skills to do this.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom and versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
